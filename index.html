About

I study sustainable policy, energy systems, and economic development, with a focus on how financial and institutional mechanisms shape access to sustainable infrastructure. My research examines how policy design, market incentives, and technological adoption influence environmental outcomes in underserved communities.

I interned with the Irrigation Department in the Government of Punjab in Punjab, where I developed an algorithm to rank agricultural households by water productivity to inform water rationing policy. Previously, I interned at Clime Finance, an NBFC, where I studied green lending models. Since 2022, I have been active in my role as the founder of Finance Frontier, an initiative that works to democratize financial services and education for low-income women and students.
